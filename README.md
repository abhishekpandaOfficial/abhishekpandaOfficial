I architect and build production-grade software across AI, cloud, full-stack engineering, and DevOps. My work sits at the intersection of autonomous systems, product design, platform engineering, and practical business value.

I focus on products that do more than demo well. They reason, automate, organize, compare, summarize, and help people make better decisions.
